About Me

Hossein is a researcher with a deep interest in Artificial Intelligence and Blockchain, motivated by a desire to explore the potential of open-source and decentralized technologies. He holds a Bachelor's in Computer Software Engineering and a Master's in Artificial Intelligence, and he continued his academic journey in Ph.D. In Data Science with a focus on Cyber Intelligence and Security orientation at the University of Salerno, Italy. With over ten years of experience in software development and digital marketing, Hossein continually seeks to grow his expertise, especially in Python, Blockchain, and PHP programming, as well as in data science, analysis, storage, and management. He is enthusiastic about applying his skills and combining knowledge from diverse fields to contribute to collaborative and interdisciplinary projects. Hossein is grateful for every opportunity to learn from new tools and technologies, and he approaches his work with humility and a commitment to making a positive impact in the fields of AI and Blockchain. Hossein is passionate about collaborative work, believing in the power of shared learning and the value of exchanging expertise within a team. He thrives in idea generation, developing concepts, actively participating in brainstorming sessions, and taking on responsibilities that enhance project efficiency and outcomes.
